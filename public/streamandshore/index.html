What is God?

God is love, not only as a feeling, but as the ground of all being.

What is love?

Love is open. Love is giving. Love is unconditional, free from desire.

What is the Holy Trinity?

The Holy Trinity embodies different aspects of God as love. The Father is love’s ultimate potential; the Son, Jesus Christ, is love’s greatest manifestation; and the Holy Spirit is the bond that connects all things. All are one with God.

What is the self?

The self is a stream of causes and conditions. It is impermanent, dependent, and belongs to all others.

What is the soul?

The soul is the Holy Spirit that bonds all things in love.

What is sin?

Sin is the illusion of separateness, a denial of love and of the Holy Spirit.

How do I practice unconditional love, free from desire?

By emptying the ego, following the teachings of Christ and the Buddha.
